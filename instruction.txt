Welcome to the DocFinder frontend repository! This guide focuses specifically on building the content sections for the app/page.jsx of our Next.js application. Your existing layout.jsx (with its Header and Footer) will wrap these components.

âš¡ Core Sections & Component Breakdown (for app/page.jsx)
Each of these components should be developed individually and then imported sequentially into your app/page.jsx from the components/home directory.
1. Hero Section (Top Fold Content)
Component: components/home/HeroSection.jsx
Description: The welcoming content directly below the header. It should feature a main headline and sub-headline.
Content:
Main Headline: "Helping people live longer, healthier lives." (Adjust as needed)
Sub-headline: A concise paragraph outlining DocFinder's mission and benefits for Bangladesh.
Note: The three colored cards from the original DocPlanner page are visually integrated but functionally a separate component (BenefitsGrid) placed directly after this text in app/page.jsx.
Styling Notes:
Main Headline: Large, bold, text-cyan-600.
Sub-headline: text-[#667070].
Layout: Centered text, ample vertical spacing.
2. Benefits Grid (The Three Cards)
Component: components/home/BenefitsGrid.jsx
Description: A grid of three distinct cards that highlight the benefits for different user groups (Patients, Doctors, Clinics). These will have dynamic backgrounds and content. This section will visually appear integrated with the HeroSection content due to the original design's layout.
Content for Each Card (Example for "For patients"):
Title: "For patients"
Description: "Find a doctor by specialist, location, or symptom. Book and manage appointments effortlessly in Bangladesh."
Action: A Button component with text like "Choose a doctor".
Visuals: Each card should have a distinct, visually appealing background color.
Styling Notes:
Titles: text-white on colored backgrounds.
Description: text-white on colored backgrounds.
Button: Use your components/common/Button.jsx, styled to complement the card's background.
Suggested Colors (Tailwind classes):
Card 1 (bg-cyan-600): Represents primary brand.
Card 2 (bg-blue-600): Complementary color for another segment.
Card 3 (bg-teal-600): Another complementary color for the third segment.
3. Global Presence / Trust Section (Adapted for Local Context)
Component: components/home/GlobalPresenceSection.jsx
Description: This section builds trust by emphasizing local reach and partnerships within Bangladesh. It replaces the "We are a global company..." section.
Content:
Headline: "We are building a healthier future in Bangladesh."
Sub-headline: "Committed to connecting patients with trusted healthcare providers across the nation."
Logos/Icons Grid: Display 3-4 significant cities or regions within Bangladesh where DocFinder operates (e.g., Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, Khulna). Use custom SVG icons or simple text labels for these.
Styling Notes:
Headline: text-[#667070], bold, possibly larger font size.
Sub-headline: text-[#667070].
Logos/Icons: Arrange in a responsive grid.
4. Statistics Section (Impact & Scale in Bangladesh)
Component: components/home/StatsSection.jsx
Description: Displays key metrics to demonstrate DocFinder's impact and scale within the Bangladeshi healthcare landscape.
Content:
Headline: "Leading the change in healthcare for Bangladesh."
Statistics Grid (4 items):
Metric 1: E.g., "50+ Cities": "Active in Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, and more."
Metric 2: E.g., "100,000+ Appointments": "Successfully scheduled appointments."
Metric 3: E.g., "5,000+ Active Doctors": "Trusted doctors on our platform."
Metric 4: E.g., "200,000+ Active Patients": "Patients connected to care."
Styling Notes:
Headline: Large, text-cyan-600, bold.
Stats items: Clear, prominent numbers with descriptive text underneath. Use text-[#667070].
Background: A subtle, lighter background color (e.g., bg-gray-50 or bg-blue-50) to differentiate it.
5. Local Presence Grid (Images of Bangladeshi Cities)
Component: components/home/LocalPresenceGrid.jsx
Description: A visually appealing grid showcasing beautiful images of major cities or regions in Bangladesh where DocFinder offers services.
Content:
Headline: "Improving lives in Bangladeshi cities."
Grid of City Cards: For each city (e.g., Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, Khulna, Rajshahi, Barisal):
Image of the city (use next/image).
City Name overlay.
A primary Button component with text "Find a doctor" that would link to a filtered search page for that city.
Styling Notes:
Headline: text-[#667070].
Images: Ensure high-quality, relevant images for Bangladeshi cities. Use next/image for performance.
Overlays: Clear white text, potentially a semi-transparent dark background for readability.
Buttons: Button component with bg-cyan-600 and text-white.
6. Call to Action (CTA) Section (Final Prompt)
Component: components/home/CtaSection.jsx
Description: The final call to action, encouraging users to begin their journey with DocFinder.
Content:
Headline: "Ready to find your doctor?"
Sub-headline: "Join DocFinder today and experience seamless healthcare in Bangladesh."
Action Button: A prominent Button component with text "Find a Doctor Now".
Styling Notes:
Headline: text-[#667070].
Sub-headline: text-[#667070].
Button: Primary Button component, bg-cyan-600, text-white, bold.
